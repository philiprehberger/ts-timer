## 0.1.0 — 2026-03-20

### Added

- `timer()` with `elapsed`, `stop`, `reset`, `lap`, `laps`, and `format` methods
- `measure(fn)` for synchronous timing
- `measureAsync(fn)` for asynchronous timing
- `benchmark(fn, options)` with mean, median, p95, p99, min, max, and ops/sec
- `formatDuration(ms)` utility for human-readable duration strings
- Full TypeScript type exports
